Environment

☀️ Spain's Climate

🌊 Mediterranean

Mild winters and hot, humid summers. Perfect for coastal lovers. (Barcelona, Valencia)

🌵 The South

The warmest region. Summers can exceed 40°C. Mild and sunny winters. (Malaga, Seville)

🧊 The North

Green, lush, and rainy. Similar to northern Iran. (Bilbao, Asturias)

🏜️ Central Spain

Hot, dry summers and cold winters. Significant day/night shifts. (Madrid)

🏖️ The Islands

Subtropical perfection. Average 20°C - 26°C year-round. (Canary Islands)

Cost of Living

Security

Healthcare

World-class infrastructure offering extensive public and private care.
